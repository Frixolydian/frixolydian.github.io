LofiBot is a bot which generates random chord progressions and melodies, along with a beat. It also randomly creates a song title and posts a gif.

You can share the music you are listening to with others by clicking on the copy link button.

Play along the bot by pressing these keys:

QWERTYUI
ASDFGHJK
ZXCVBNM,

(pro tip: mute the melody by pressing spacebar or clicking the piano button)
